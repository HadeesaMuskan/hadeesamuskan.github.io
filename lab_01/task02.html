Breakfast Scrambled Eggs
  • 4 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter for cooking

1. Crack eggs into a bowl and whisk.

2. Add salt and pepper to taste.

3. Heat butter in a pan and pour in the eggs.

4. Stir until cooked and serve hot.
Lunch Chicken Salad
  • Grilled chicken breast
  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Salad dressing

1. Grill the chicken breast until fully cooked.

2. Chop the chicken and mix with greens, tomatoes, and cucumbers.

3. Add your favorite salad dressing and toss well.

4. Serve and enjoy!
